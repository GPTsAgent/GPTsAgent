# Code of Conduct

GPTsAgent discussion should stay practical, evidence-based, and respectful.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus critique on the work and its risks.
- Keep security reports sanitized.
- Respect sandbox and privacy boundaries.
- Assume public issues are public.
- Use precise claims and validation labels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Posting secrets, tokens, credential bodies, or private data.
- Encouraging credential theft, malware, unauthorized access, evasion, or destructive automation.
- Pressuring maintainers to overclaim capabilities that were not verified.

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove unsafe content and restrict participation when needed to protect users and the project.
